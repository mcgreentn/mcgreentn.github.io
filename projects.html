Projects

Research & AI

The AtDelfi System
Automatically Designing Legible, Full Instructions — the beginnings of my thesis research on automated video game tutorial generation.
MicroRCT
An open source simulator inspired by RollerCoaster Tycoon. Place rides and shops to maximize profit; built as a testbed for AI agents.
The GDMC Challenge
Generative design in Minecraft — a competition to build AI that creates functional settlements in procedurally generated environments.
The GVG-AI Framework
An easy, efficient way to test AI against a large variety of 2D video games. I co-developed the Rule-Generation Track with colleagues.
